About Us

The Budva Chamber of Commerce is a professional, independent, business association with a rich tradition, founded in 2012, representing the interests of small and large entrepreneurs for the economic and general development of the municipality of Budva, Montenegro. The common mission of all its members is to direct and carry out activities to support the economic and general development of the region. Among the most important tasks of the chamber are to foster the development and promotion of entrepreneurship and profitable economic indicators to develop and maintain a healthy socio-economic climate in the local community, presenting business opportunities, contributing to the strengthening of good business practices and business ethics, as well as educating and training its members in according to the demand of the economy.
