Introduction to Membership

Join the Budva Chamber, the largest business association of the municipality of Budva, and strengthen your business and local community.

Membership in the Budva Chamber is a business involvement, and like all investments, a good business owner expects positive results. This is why Budva Chamber provides a range of benefits designed to increase the profitability of your business and build a better community.

The Budva Chamber of Commerce offers four levels of membership per year, each suitable for different types, sizes and circumstances of business in Budva municipality. Members often join, become familiar with the Chamber, and then progress to a higher level of membership.

Core Benefits:

  • SEO (Search Engine Optimized) placement in the online chamber business directory on the chamber website
  • Networking opportunities throughout the year with member prices for programs, meetings and branded events
  • Referral of the chamber to your business / organization
  • The ability to post your company announcements in the news for members on the Chamber website and on social media
  • “Proud Member” Online Certificate
  • Advertising and sponsorship opportunities (print and online)
  • Targeting the launch of the Chamber to explore the benefits and get to know other members
  • Invitations to seminars, trainings and programs designed to keep you updated on key topics, upcoming events and unique opportunities
  • Possibility to post vacancies on the Board of Employment of the Chamber
  • Discounts (i.e. Budva Drug Card, Office Depot merchandise, services and certificates of origin)
  • Ability to post corporate events to the community calendar

Membership Levels

Non-profit: €100

This membership level is only viable for non-profit organizations or companies with 3 or less employees and includes all Core Benefits and those listed below.

Included Benefits:

  • One registration to Chamber Launch
  • Two registrations for Business After Hours event (participation in the opening of the chamber is required)

Bronze: €200

This membership level is designed to help you increase visibility in the business community and includes all Core Benefits and those listed below.

Included Benefits:

Engagement:

  • Two registrations for one Business After Hours event
  • Two registrations for the Annual Legislative Admission
  • Two registrations to Chamber Launch
  • Invitation to participate in Chamber committees or working groups (i.e. Small Business Committee, Health Systems Reform, Natural Resources Business Council)
  • Invitation to participate in the Business Women Forum

Exposure:

  • Expanded website listing in the Chamber's business directory (organization name and contact name, additional category, logo, two keywords, website and map)
  • In the spotlight of the members (featured on social media platforms)

Silver: €400

This membership level provides you with strategic networking and access to interact with key decision-makers and peers, learn best practices that drive community development, and includes all Core benefits and a wide range of opportunities for enhanced engagement and disclosure.

Included Benefits:

Engagement:

  • Four registrations to each Business After Hours event
  • Four registrations to Annual Legislative Reception
  • Two registrations to Chamber Launch
  • Invitation to participate in Chamber committees or working groups (i.e. Small Business Committee, Health Systems Reform, Natural Resources Business Council)
  • One registration a quarter to a Policy Forum (i.e. Cybersecurity, Workforce, Water, Education, Infrastructure)
  • Invitation to Circle of Influence events

Exposure:

  • Expanded website listing in the Chamber's business directory (organization name and contact name, additional category, logo, two keywords, website and map)
  • In the spotlight of the members (featured on social media platforms)

Gold: €1,000

This membership level increases your influence among elected officials and access to your customers and peers to help develop policies and positions on key issues affecting the Budva community, and includes all Core benefits and a wide range of enhanced communication and disclosure options.

Included Benefits:

Engagement:

  • Unlimited registrations to each Business After Hours event
  • Unlimited registrations to Annual Legislative Reception
  • Invitation to participate in Chamber committees or working groups (i.e. Small Business Committee, Health Systems Reform, Natural Resources Business Council)
  • Two registrations to each Policy Forum (i.e. Cybersecurity, Workforce, Water Education, Infrastructure)
  • Invitation to Circle of Influence events
  • Host CEO Roundtables (space requirements apply)
  • Invitation to Business Champion Awards

Sponsorship:

  • Sponsor a Starter Entrepreneur or Nonprofit with Basic Membership Level for One Year (€ 500)

Exposure:

  • Prime-positioned website listing in the Chamber's business directory (organization and contact name, three categories, logo, seven keywords, website and map)
  • In the spotlight of the members (featured on social media platforms)
  • Advertising of the company on the website of the Chamber (based on availability)
