Color Scheme

Primary Secondary Accent 1 Accent 2 Accent 3
[#FEFEFE] [#170030] [#F5CF87] [#433156] [9387A0]

The primary color will be used for headings and paragraphs as a light background against which dark text will be displayed and also as light text against a dark background.

The secondary color will fill background of the menu, footer and also will be applied to display dark text on a light background.

Accent 1 will display heading text on a dark background as well as menu text and also will be used for the color of the buttons.

Accent 2 will be used to display the text of paragraphs placed against a light background.

Accent 3 will be used only as an additional background for the tablet and mobile versions of the site pages.
