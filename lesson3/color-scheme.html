Color Scheme

Primary Secondary Accent 1 Accent 2 Accent 3
[#FEFEFE] [#003959] [#006199] [#F0FFFF] [#E94E00]

The primary color will be used for headings and paragraphs as a light background against which dark text will be displayed and also as light text against a dark background.

The secondary color will be applied to paragraphs as a dark background on which to display light text, and also as dark text on a light background.

Accent 1 will display heading text on a light background as well as menu text.

Accent 2 fill the background of the menu.

Orange will be used for the color of the button and links.
